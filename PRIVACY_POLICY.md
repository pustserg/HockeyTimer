# Privacy Policy for HockeyTimer

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## Overview

HockeyTimer is a hockey shift timer application that operates entirely offline on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** HockeyTimer:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not collect usage analytics
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Does not transmit any data to external servers

## Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This includes:

- Timer configurations (shift intervals, game duration)
- Appearance preferences (colors, backgrounds)
- Sound and haptic settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Third-Party Services

HockeyTimer does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

HockeyTimer does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/pustserg/HockeyTimer).
